A little bit about us
At the HVHS Coffee House we are committed to providing a quality experience in all aspects of coffee. The HVHS Coffee House is an ongoing initiative at school where we attempt to not only serve great coffee to customers, but pass on our knowledge to the next generation. Our training program aims to set people up for success with training and insight into the barista industry.
Our six week training program goes over all the basics you need to excel in a future barista position. We cover the service and upkeep of a cafe environment such as...
  • How to prepare an espresso shot
  • How to steam milk
  • Service with a smile
  • Keeping the cafe clean and tidy
Contact your deans for more information about applying. We start taking applications at the start of the year. If you are committed and willing to learn, this could be the position for you.
